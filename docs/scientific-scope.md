# Scientific Scope

COSMOS-CQA focuses on citizen-assisted quality assurance for cosmology artifacts.

Primary scope:

- CMB and weak-lensing tile review;
- artifact taxonomy and severity labeling;
- deterministic visual and audio sidecars;
- expert adjudication workflows;
- inter-rater and reliability metrics;
- provenance manifests and tile passports;
- validation reports for reproducibility review.

Out of scope:

- final cosmological parameter inference;
- clinical, medical, regulatory, or safety-critical decision-making;
- production scientific claims without independent expert validation;
- redistribution of third-party datasets outside their terms.

The project should make artifact review more reproducible and inspectable. It should not claim to replace expert cosmology pipelines.
