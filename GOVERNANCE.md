# Governance

COSMOS-CQA is owned and stewarded by AI-Bio Synergy Holdings LLC.

## Steward Role

The steward is responsible for:

- project direction and scope;
- license and IP policy;
- release approval;
- repository administration;
- acceptance or rejection of contributions;
- publication of official research artifacts.

## Public Participation

Researchers, educators, reviewers, and collaborators may participate through issues, discussions, pull requests, and research feedback, subject to the project license and contribution terms.

## Decision Model

Project decisions are made by the steward. Community feedback is welcome, but no public contribution or discussion creates an obligation to merge, release, support, or commercially license any feature.

## Release Policy

Official releases should include:

- version tag;
- changelog;
- source snapshot;
- SBOM when software dependencies are present;
- schema versions;
- validation or test report;
- license and notice files;
- citation metadata;
- third-party data-use notes.

## Scope Guardrails

COSMOS-CQA is a research-source workbench for artifact QA, provenance, reproducibility, and review workflows. It should not be represented as a clinical, regulatory, production, or final scientific inference system.
