# Contributing

COSMOS-CQA welcomes research-oriented discussion, issues, documentation improvements, test cases, and proposed patches that support the project scope.

## Before Contributing

By contributing, you agree that:

- you have the right to submit the contribution;
- your contribution may be used under the COSMOS-CQA Research-Only Public License;
- AI-Bio Synergy Holdings LLC may use, modify, distribute, sublicense, and relicense your contribution as described in `LICENSE.md`;
- you will not submit confidential, proprietary, export-controlled, regulated, personally identifying, or restricted third-party materials unless you have written authorization.

## Contribution Scope

Good contributions include:

- bug reports with reproduction steps;
- accessibility findings;
- provenance and schema improvements;
- deterministic test cases;
- documentation clarifications;
- research examples using public or properly licensed data.

Out of scope without prior written approval:

- commercial product integrations;
- production-hosted service changes;
- redistribution of restricted third-party datasets;
- medical, clinical, regulatory, or safety-critical workflows;
- claims that COSMOS-CQA validates scientific conclusions beyond documented QA outputs.

## Development Flow

1. Open an issue for substantial changes.
2. Keep changes focused.
3. Include tests or verification notes when behavior changes.
4. Preserve license, notice, attribution, and provenance metadata.
5. Avoid adding large binary artifacts to Git history.

## Data Contributions

Do not commit raw third-party datasets unless their terms explicitly permit redistribution. Prefer manifests, checksums, small synthetic examples, and documented download instructions.
