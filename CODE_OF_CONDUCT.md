# Code of Conduct

COSMOS-CQA is a research-source project for scientific and educational collaboration. Participants are expected to communicate respectfully, stay on topic, and help preserve a constructive research environment.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, abusive language, doxxing, spam, deliberate misinformation about project capabilities, or attempts to pressure contributors into violating data, license, or institutional restrictions.

The steward may remove content, close issues, reject contributions, or limit participation to protect the project and its contributors.
