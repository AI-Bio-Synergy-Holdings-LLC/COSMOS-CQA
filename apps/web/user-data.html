User Data Notice

Local-first research workflow data.

The public portal is a static site. The browser workbench is designed for local-first research artifacts, imports, exports, downloads, and replay evidence.

Current public data behavior

No account system

The hosted static portal does not provide public accounts, login, subscriptions, or server-side workspaces.

Local imports and exports

Files selected in the browser are used for local workflow inspection. Exported reports, sessions, CSVs, and evidence bundles are downloaded to the user's environment.

Browser state

The workbench may use browser-local state for demo/session behavior. Clear site data in your browser if you want to remove local state.

External services

Links to GitHub, email, public science references, or other domains are handled by those external services under their own terms.

Contact email

If you email the developer contact route, your email client and mail providers process the message. Use non-confidential summaries only. Do not send personal health information, regulated data, restricted third-party data, private keys, credentials, private source code, or confidential diligence materials through public email.
