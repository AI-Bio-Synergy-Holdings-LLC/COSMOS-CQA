Contact

Developer and public research route.

Use this contact route for COSMOS-CQA public research questions, accessibility feedback, documentation issues, release artifact questions, safety concerns, security disclosure routing, and responsible routing.

Email

Send non-confidential COSMOS-CQA inquiries to:

cosmos-cqa-developer@ai-biosynergyholdings.com

Do not send confidential information, restricted datasets, credentials, private keys, personal health information, regulated data, private source code, valuation materials, transaction terms, or diligence materials through public email. For vulnerabilities, begin with a minimal non-confidential summary and indicate whether private reproduction details are available on request.

Good first messages

  • Public research or educational use questions.
  • Accessibility and usability feedback.
  • Safety or audio/visual use-boundary concerns that do not require sensitive details.
  • Security disclosure questions that do not include public exploit details.
  • Documentation corrections.
  • Reproducibility or release artifact questions.
  • Responsible reporting of public portal issues that do not expose sensitive details.
